Helping Hands Community Hospital

Our vision is to build healthy, prosperous, self-reliant communities across modern Nepal.
We see all of Nepal as our community. We welcome patients from all 77districts. In fact, only 11% of our patients come from Kathmandu. Our hospital is on a mission to provide high quality health care to the poor and needy, orphans and old people in both urban and rural areas. More than that, we teach our patients to care for themselves and their loved ones whenever possible.

Message from Executive Chairman

Helping Hands Community Hospital has been established on March ,2008 with 100 bedded multi specialty hospital with aim of providing high standard medical care to needy people of Nepal.
We provide community based health services in rural as well as urban area and try to support the suffering of the economically deprived people of the Community.

Dr Gupta Bahadur Shrestha
Executive Chairman
